1. Export your Skillplan from EVEMon to an XML file.
  2. Open iTunes.
  3. Click on your iPhone/iPad and open the Apps tab.
  4. Select EVEUniverse from the File Sharing section.
  5. Add exported skill plan to Documents list.
  6. In the EVEUniverse App. on your iPhone/iPad select Import Skill Plan from EVEMon in the menu.
  7. Select exported skill plan.
  8. Click Import menu item and select a method of importing.
  9. Done.
